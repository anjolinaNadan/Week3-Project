About TechConnect

TechConnect is a a tech retailer company specializing in selling the latest gadgets, electronics, and cutting-edge tech products. TechConnect was founded by the CEO Rachel Barrett in 1995, and has since been one of the largest retail companies in New Zealand. There are currently 3 TechConnect companies located around New Zealand.
